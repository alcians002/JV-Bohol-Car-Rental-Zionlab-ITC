Our Journey

From a small family operation to one of Bohol's most trusted car rental services — here's how we got here.

2015 — The Beginning

Started with just 3 vehicles and a passion for hospitality. Served our first tourists arriving at Tagbilaran Port.

2018 — Fleet Expansion

Grew to 10+ vehicles including sedans, MPVs, and our first pickup trucks. Introduced airport pickup service.

2020 — Going Digital

Launched online booking through Messenger and our website. Adapted safety protocols during uncertain times.

2023 — Premium Fleet

Added brand-new Toyota Vios, Innova, and Xpander Cross models. Reached 5,000+ happy customers milestone.

2026 — Today & Beyond

Continuously expanding with the latest models, improved systems, and the same family values that started it all.
